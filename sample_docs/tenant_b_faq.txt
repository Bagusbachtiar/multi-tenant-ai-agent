=== GRANDEUR HOTEL — EVENTS AND IN-ROOM DINING FAQ

This document covers frequently asked questions about Grandeur Hotel's event services, in-room dining, facilities, and policies. For immediate assistance, dial the concierge at extension 0 or WhatsApp our guest services at +62 811-2233-4455.

=== FAQ — ROOM SERVICE AND IN-ROOM DINING

Q: What are the in-room dining hours?
A: In-room dining is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week including public holidays. Breakfast is served from 06:00–11:00, lunch from 11:00–15:00, and dinner from 18:00–23:00. Outside these hours, our All-Day Dining snack and light meal menu is available at any time.

Q: How do I place a room service order?
A: You can order by dialing extension 24 from your room phone, through the GrandeurStay mobile app (available on iOS and Android), or via the QR code menu placed on your room desk. Orders are confirmed via app notification or a call-back from our team.

Q: How long does room service delivery take?
A: Typical delivery time is 25–40 minutes for hot meals. Beverages and cold items typically arrive within 15–20 minutes. During peak hours (07:00–09:00 and 12:00–13:30), add approximately 10–15 minutes. We will notify you if your order is expected to take longer than 45 minutes.

Q: Is there a delivery charge for room service?
A: No delivery charge for in-room dining. All listed prices are inclusive of tax and service charge.

Q: Can I request a specific chef preparation or variation?
A: Yes. Please inform the room service team of any special preparation requests when ordering. We can accommodate most variations including cooking method preferences, sauce on the side, ingredient substitutions, or portion adjustments for children.

Q: Does Grandeur Hotel serve alcohol?
A: No. Grandeur Hotel does not serve or allow alcohol on the premises in accordance with our halal hospitality policy. All beverages on our menu are non-alcoholic. If you bring personal alcohol to your room, please note that consuming it in public areas of the hotel is not permitted.

=== FAQ — DIETARY REQUIREMENTS AND ALLERGIES

Q: Is all food at Grandeur Hotel halal?
A: Yes. All food and beverages served at Grandeur Hotel are fully halal-certified by MUI (Certificate No. GRND-HTL-2023-0201). No pork or pork derivatives are served anywhere in the hotel. Our suppliers are also MUI-certified.

Q: Can you accommodate vegetarian guests?
A: Yes. Vegetarian options are available across all menus and are marked with (V). We can also prepare vegetarian-only buffet stations for events. Please indicate vegetarian pax count when booking.

Q: What about vegan guests?
A: Vegan options are available with at least 2 hours advance notice for room service and 3 days for event catering. Please speak to our team to review which dishes can be made fully vegan.

Q: I have a severe nut allergy. Is it safe to order?
A: Our kitchen uses peanut oil as a cooking medium for several dishes. We maintain a separate cooking station for nut-free preparations using sunflower oil. However, we cannot guarantee a fully nut-free kitchen environment. Please notify our team before ordering so we can take maximum precautions. Guests with severe, life-threatening allergies are advised to carry personal medication (EpiPen) at all times.

Q: Do you have gluten-free options?
A: Yes. Most grilled proteins, rice dishes, salads, and soups can be prepared gluten-free. Soy sauce is replaced with gluten-free tamari on request. Pastries and bread-based items are not gluten-free by default. Please ask our team for a full gluten-free menu list.

=== FAQ — EVENT BOOKINGS AND RESERVATIONS

Q: How do I book an event venue?
A: Contact our Events Division at events@grandeurhotel.co.id or call +62 21-5678-9012 ext. 300. We will arrange a site inspection and send you a formal proposal within 2 business days. To confirm, sign the event agreement and pay a 30% deposit.

Q: What deposit is required to secure an event date?
A: A 30% non-refundable deposit is required to confirm and hold your event date. The remaining 70% is due 7 days before the event. For events above IDR 500,000,000, a payment schedule can be arranged.

Q: How far in advance should I book?
A: For large events (Grand Ballroom, weddings, galas), we recommend booking 3–6 months in advance. Popular dates (year-end, Eid, Valentine's Day) may be booked up to 12 months ahead. Meeting rooms and smaller spaces can often be booked within 2–3 weeks.

Q: Can I visit the venue before booking?
A: Yes. We encourage site visits Monday–Friday 09:00–17:00 and Saturdays 09:00–13:00. Our events coordinator will walk you through the space, discuss layout options, and answer questions. Contact us to schedule.

Q: Can I bring my own decorations or third-party suppliers?
A: External floral decorators, photographers, and entertainment vendors are welcome with prior approval from our events team. All external vendors must comply with hotel safety guidelines and check in at the events office before setup. Open flames, nails in walls, and glitter are not permitted.

=== FAQ — CANCELLATIONS AND CHANGES

Q: What is your event cancellation policy?
A: Cancellations must be made in writing to events@grandeurhotel.co.id.
- Cancel 30+ days before event: 30% deposit forfeited, remaining balance refunded in full.
- Cancel 14–29 days before: 50% of total contract value charged.
- Cancel 7–13 days before: 75% of total contract value charged.
- Cancel less than 7 days: 100% of total contract value charged.
- Force majeure (proven government order, natural disaster): full rescheduling option at no cost.

Q: Can I reschedule my event instead of cancelling?
A: Yes. One free rescheduling is permitted if requested 14+ days before the original event date, subject to venue availability. A second rescheduling incurs a 10% administration fee.

Q: Can I increase or decrease the number of guests?
A: Guest count changes must be communicated 5 business days before the event. Increases are accommodated subject to venue capacity. Decreases of more than 20% of the confirmed pax count may result in an adjustment fee.

=== FAQ — AV AND TECHNICAL

Q: What AV equipment is included in venue rental?
A: All venue rentals include projector or screen, PA system, wireless microphones (number varies by venue), and basic event lighting. High-speed dedicated Wi-Fi (200 Mbps) is also included.

Q: Can I stream my event online?
A: Yes. Our technical team offers hybrid event streaming to Zoom, Microsoft Teams, or YouTube Live at IDR 5,000,000 per session. This includes a dedicated technician and camera setup. Please request this at least 7 days before your event.

Q: Do you provide a technician for AV support during the event?
A: A standby technician is available for all events. For events requiring dedicated full-time AV support (concerts, product launches, hybrid events), a dedicated technician is available at IDR 1,500,000/day.

Q: Is the Wi-Fi suitable for video conferencing?
A: Yes. Each event room has a dedicated bandwidth allocation. Cerulean Meeting Room and Sapphire Boardroom include Zoom/Teams-compatible video conferencing systems with 4K cameras and directional microphones built in.

=== FAQ — PARKING AND GENERAL FACILITIES

Q: Is parking available for event guests?
A: Yes. We have 800 underground parking spaces. Event guests receive 3 hours of complimentary parking with validation from the concierge. After 3 hours, the rate is IDR 10,000/hour. Valet parking is available at IDR 50,000 per vehicle.

Q: Is the venue accessible for guests with disabilities?
A: Yes. All event floors are accessible via dedicated lifts. Accessible restrooms are available on all event levels. Wheelchair-friendly seating arrangements can be requested. Sign language interpretation is available with 7 days advance notice.

Q: Is there a prayer room (musholla) available?
A: Yes. Prayer rooms with ablution facilities are located on Level 2 (near the main lobby) and Level 7 (near the Grand Ballroom foyer). Both are available to guests and event attendees at all times.

Q: Can guests stay overnight after an event?
A: Yes. We offer preferential room rates for event attendees. Contact our reservations team at reservations@grandeurhotel.co.id at least 1 week before the event to arrange a group room block at a discounted rate.

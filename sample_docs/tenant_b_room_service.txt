=== GRANDEUR HOTEL — IN-ROOM DINING MENU

Welcome to Grandeur Hotel In-Room Dining. Available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. To place an order, dial extension 24 from your room phone or order via the GrandeurStay app. Delivery time is approximately 25–40 minutes. All prices are in IDR and are exclusive of 21% government tax and service charge (already included in the listed price).

For dietary requests, special preparations, or allergy accommodations, please speak with our In-Room Dining team at extension 24 before placing your order.

=== SPECIAL DIETARY AND ALLERGY POLICY

Grandeur Hotel In-Room Dining can accommodate a wide range of dietary requirements including:
- Vegetarian (lacto-ovo): clearly marked on menu with (V)
- Vegan: available on request, minimum 2 hours notice
- Gluten-free: available for most dishes, inform team when ordering
- Nut-free: our kitchen uses peanut oil — nut-free versions prepared in separate area on request
- Diabetic-friendly: low-sugar options available, ask team for recommendations
- Low-sodium: available on request for most dishes

All food served at Grandeur Hotel is halal. We do not serve pork or alcohol in any form. Our halal certification (MUI No. GRND-HTL-2023-0201) is displayed at the front desk.

=== SILVER PACKAGE — FULL DETAILS

Target events: casual office lunches, birthday parties, small community events, seminars under 100 pax.

The Silver package is our entry-level offering designed for informal occasions where budget efficiency is the priority without sacrificing authentic Indonesian taste. All Silver menus feature a rotating selection of home-style dishes prepared fresh on the day of the event.

What is included:
- 2 main course dishes (client selects from Silver menu)
- 1 steamed white rice (unlimited serving)
- 1 vegetable side dish
- Soto ayam (chicken turmeric broth, served in individual cups)
- Seasonal sliced fruit
- Sweet iced tea and mineral water
- Disposable plates, bowls, cups, spoons, and forks
- Standard chafing dishes and serving utensils
- 1 serving staff per 50 pax
- Delivery within 30 km radius

What is NOT included: dining tables, chairs, canopy, tablecloths, printed menus.

Best paired with: a simple venue like a meeting room, community hall, or open yard.

=== GOLD PACKAGE — FULL DETAILS

Target events: corporate luncheons, anniversary celebrations, family gatherings of 50–300 pax, formal seminars, product launches.

The Gold package is our most popular tier, offering a well-balanced spread that satisfies both traditional Indonesian palates and guests less familiar with local cuisine. It includes more variety and presentation upgrades compared to Silver.

What is included:
- 3 main course dishes (client selects from Gold menu)
- 2 rice varieties: nasi putih + nasi goreng special
- 2 vegetable side dishes
- Soto ayam (upgrade to sup buntut available at +IDR 15,000/pax)
- Chocolate pudding and kolak pisang (banana compote)
- Sweet iced tea, fresh orange juice, mineral water
- Disposable plates, bowls, cups, spoons, forks, and tissues
- Standard chafing dishes with fuel backup
- 1 serving staff per 50 pax
- Delivery within 30 km radius

Optional upgrades for Gold:
- Live sate station: +IDR 25,000/pax
- Printed menu cards (per table): +IDR 150,000 per 10 tables
- Extra serving staff: +IDR 250,000 per additional staff member

=== PLATINUM PACKAGE — FULL DETAILS

Target events: weddings, gala dinners, government functions, high-end corporate events, celebrations of 100–2,000 pax.

The Platinum package is Dapur Nusantara's flagship offering, designed for events where every detail matters. This package includes our highest-quality ingredients, premium presentation, and full-service staffing.

What is included:
- 4 main course dishes (client selects from Platinum menu)
- 3 rice varieties: nasi putih, nasi goreng special, nasi kuning
- 3 vegetable side dishes
- 2 soups: sup buntut (oxtail) and soto betawi (Betawi beef coconut broth)
- 3 dessert options including caramel milk pudding and premium fruit platter
- Full drink selection: iced tea, orange juice, choice of fruit juice, and mineral water
- Premium disposable ware (heavier-duty plates, proper serving bowls)
- Premium chafing dishes with decorative stands
- 2 serving staff per 50 pax
- 1 head waiter per event
- White tablecloths and skirting for serving tables
- Dedicated event liaison (on-call from D-3 to event end)
- Delivery within 50 km radius (extended range)

=== TERMS AND CONDITIONS

1. BOOKING CONFIRMATION
   A booking is confirmed only upon receipt of the signed service agreement and a 50% deposit payment. Verbal or chat-based confirmations do not constitute a binding agreement.

2. CHANGES TO ORDER
   Changes to pax count, menu selection, or service date must be made in writing (WhatsApp or email) no later than 3 business days before the event. Changes to pax count exceeding ±20% of the original order will require re-quotation.

3. CANCELLATION POLICY
   - Cancellation 7+ days before event: full 50% deposit refunded.
   - Cancellation 3–6 days before event: 25% of deposit refunded.
   - Cancellation less than 3 days before event: no refund.
   - Force majeure cancellations (natural disaster, government order): full deposit refund or free rescheduling.

4. FOOD SAFETY DISCLAIMER
   Dapur Nusantara is not liable for any adverse reactions caused by undisclosed allergies. Clients are responsible for informing us of all dietary restrictions and allergies no later than 3 days before the event.

5. PROPERTY AND EQUIPMENT
   All equipment provided by Dapur Nusantara (chafing dishes, serving stands, linen) remains our property and must be returned in clean condition. Damage to equipment will be charged at replacement cost.

6. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
   Any disputes shall first be resolved through amicable negotiation. If unresolved within 14 days, disputes shall be submitted to the South Jakarta District Court (Pengadilan Negeri Jakarta Selatan) as the agreed jurisdiction.

7. GOVERNING LAW
   This agreement is governed by the laws of the Republic of Indonesia.

=== HALAL AND DIETARY INFORMATION

Dapur Nusantara Catering is fully halal-certified. All meat products are sourced from MUI-certified suppliers. No pork or alcohol is used in any of our dishes or cooking processes.

Vegetarian options:
- Full vegetarian meals available on request at same package price
- Vegan options available with 3-day advance notice
- All vegetable dishes are cooked separately from meat dishes

Allergen information:
- Peanuts: present in gado-gado and some sauces — inform us if any guest has peanut allergy
- Shellfish: present in Gold and Platinum packages by default — can be substituted
- Gluten: soy sauce used in many dishes — gluten-free soy sauce available on request (+IDR 5,000/pax)
- Tree nuts: not used in standard recipes

Guests with severe allergies must notify us at least 48 hours before the event.

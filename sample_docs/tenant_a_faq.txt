=== DAPUR NUSANTARA CATERING — FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

This document answers the most common questions about ordering, delivery, dietary needs, and policies at Dapur Nusantara Catering. For questions not covered here, contact us at +62 812-3456-7890 or order@dapurnusantara.co.id.

=== FAQ — ORDERING AND BOOKING

Q: What is the minimum order?
A: The minimum order is 50 pax for Silver and Gold packages, and 100 pax for the Platinum package. For orders below 50 pax, contact us — we may accommodate smaller orders with a small fee adjustment.

Q: How far in advance do I need to book?
A: We recommend booking at least 7 days in advance for standard orders. For Platinum package events (weddings, galas), we recommend 14–30 days. During peak season (November–February, holiday months), book at least 3–4 weeks ahead.

Q: Can I change my order after booking?
A: Yes, you can make changes up to 3 days before your event. Changes to pax count of more than 20% after booking confirmation may require re-quotation. Menu changes within the same package are allowed free of charge with 3 days notice.

Q: Do you cater to multiple locations on the same day?
A: Yes, we have multiple teams that can serve different locations simultaneously. Mention this during booking so we can coordinate logistics properly.

Q: Can I visit your kitchen before ordering?
A: Yes, kitchen visits are welcome Monday–Friday, 09:00–15:00 WIB. Please schedule at least 2 days in advance. We are proud of our hygiene standards and encourage clients to inspect our facility.

=== FAQ — DELIVERY AND SETUP

Q: Do you deliver to all areas?
A: We deliver across Greater Jakarta (Jabodetabek). For events outside this area (e.g., Bandung, Bogor, Karawang), a transport surcharge applies — typically IDR 500,000–2,000,000 depending on distance. Contact us for exact rates.

Q: What time will your team arrive to set up?
A: Our team arrives 1–2 hours before the event start time for setup. For Platinum packages, we arrive 2–3 hours early. Please ensure venue access is ready for our team at that time.

Q: Do you provide serving equipment?
A: Yes. All packages include chafing dishes, serving utensils, disposable plates, disposable cups, and napkins. Platinum package also includes tablecloths and decorative serving stands. We do not provide dining chairs or tables (only serving tables).

Q: What happens if the venue has no electricity?
A: Our chafing dishes can run on either electricity or fuel (Sterno). Inform us in advance if your venue has limited power access so we can prepare accordingly.

Q: How long does the food stay warm?
A: Food stays at serving temperature for approximately 2–3 hours using chafing dishes. For longer events, we can arrange staggered serving or food replenishment at additional cost.

=== FAQ — PAYMENT AND PRICING

Q: Are your prices negotiable?
A: Package prices are fixed per person. However, for very large orders (500+ pax) or repeat corporate clients, we offer a loyalty discount of 5–10%. Contact us to discuss.

Q: What is included in the price?
A: Package prices include food, disposable serving ware, chafing dishes, serving staff (1 per 50 pax), and delivery within 30 km of our kitchen. They do not include dining tables, chairs, or tent/canopy.

Q: Do you charge extra for weekends or public holidays?
A: Events on Sundays and public holidays incur a 10% surcharge on the total order value. Saturday events have no surcharge.

Q: Can I get an official invoice (faktur pajak)?
A: Yes. Dapur Nusantara is a registered VAT-able company (PKP). We can issue a faktur pajak for corporate clients. Please inform us at time of booking and provide your company NPWP.

Q: What currency do you accept?
A: All prices are in Indonesian Rupiah (IDR). We do not accept foreign currency directly.

=== FAQ — HALAL AND FOOD SAFETY

Q: Is Dapur Nusantara halal-certified?
A: Yes. We hold a valid MUI (Majelis Ulama Indonesia) halal certification. Our certificate number is MUI-JKTPS-2024-0087. All suppliers are also MUI-certified. We do not use pork, lard, or alcohol in any form.

Q: How is your food safety managed?
A: Our kitchen complies with BPOM food safety regulations. All chefs undergo annual food handler certification. Food temperatures are monitored from production through delivery. We do not serve food that has been out of safe temperature range.

Q: Can you accommodate vegetarian guests?
A: Yes. We can prepare fully vegetarian versions of any package at no extra cost. Please specify the number of vegetarian pax when ordering so we can prepare separate portions.

Q: Do you have vegan options?
A: Yes, vegan options are available with at least 3 days advance notice. Vegan dishes exclude dairy, eggs, and all animal-derived ingredients. Please note some sauces may contain trace amounts of fish sauce unless specifically requested otherwise.

Q: How do you handle peanut allergies?
A: We take allergies seriously. Peanuts are used in gado-gado and some sambal recipes. If any guest has a peanut allergy, inform us and we will prepare their portion in a separate area using peanut-free ingredients. We cannot guarantee a fully peanut-free kitchen environment.

=== FAQ — WEDDING AND SPECIAL EVENTS

Q: Do you handle full wedding catering?
A: Yes. We specialize in wedding catering from 100 to 2,000 pax. Our Platinum package is designed for weddings. We also offer wedding coordination packages that include floral centerpieces, table decor, and a dedicated event coordinator — contact us for a custom quote.

Q: Can you provide a live cooking station (live action)?
A: Yes. We offer live cooking stations including sate grilling, mie goreng (fried noodle), es krim, and martabak manis stations. Each live station starts from IDR 1,500,000 and requires 1–2 dedicated chefs. Minimum order still applies.

Q: Do you provide a wedding cake?
A: We partner with certified halal bakeries for wedding cakes. A 3-tier wedding cake starts from IDR 1,200,000. We can arrange tasting sessions before the event. Please note this is a separate add-on, not included in any catering package.

Q: Can you customize a menu for a cultural or themed event?
A: Yes. We can customize menus for Javanese, Sundanese, Betawi, Minang, or fusion-themed events. Customization requires at least 10 days lead time and may incur a design fee of IDR 200,000–500,000 depending on complexity.
